Alarik turns the center of the table into a long argument. This test profile points toward resilience, threat control, and a presence that rewards placing him exactly where the opponent would rather not fight.

The key is support. If he goes in alone, he can become isolated; if he advances with backup, he forces difficult decisions and opens space for the rest of the force to work on objectives.
