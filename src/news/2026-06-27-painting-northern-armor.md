Start with a dark metallic base, add a cold wash, and finish with very fine edge highlights. The result is quick, forgiving, and keeps the miniatures looking hard-worn without losing readability on the table.

To finish, use desaturated leathers and small warm points on straps, emblems, or cloth. That contrast keeps the model from collapsing into a single grey mass.
