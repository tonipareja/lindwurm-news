The summer league offers five weeks of linked games, short missions, painting goals, and small narrative modifiers. The aim is simple: even a loss should add something useful to the campaign.

To get started, publish the schedule, format, list limits, and a simple tracking sheet. After that, each week can have its own post with results, table photos, and the moments players are still talking about afterwards.
